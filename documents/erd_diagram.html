Entity Relationships

  • User: Students and Instructors (role-based)
  • Course: Courses created by Instructors
  • Enrollment: Links Students to Courses (Many-to-Many)
  • CourseContent: PDF files and materials for courses
  • Assignment: Assignments within courses
  • Submission: Student submissions for assignments
  • Grade: Grades and feedback on submissions
